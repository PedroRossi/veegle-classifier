Introduction to Indian Cooking

This introduction to Indian cooking will unravel the secrets to cooking with spices, discuss which ones are important to have in your home collection and show you techniques for getting the most flavor out of each dish. After taking this class you’ll be able to create a complete 4-course vegetarian Indian meal and a mouthwatering dessert. And the knowledge you’ll gain can be applied to any dish that utilizes these core Indian spices.
